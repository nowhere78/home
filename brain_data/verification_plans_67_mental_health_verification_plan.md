# Verification Plan - Mental health

## Verification Priority
- Accuracy first, then distinctness, then completeness.

## Minimum Verification Standard
- Cross-check major factual claims against at least two independent authoritative sources whenever feasible.
- Raise the standard further for disputed, technical, scientific, historical, or controversial claims.
- Label uncertainty, interpretation, dispute, or school differences honestly.

## Required Passes Per Batch
- Source verification pass
- Duplicate pass
- Similarity pass
- Usefulness/specificity audit

## Evidence To Record
- Source classes used
- Claims that were disputed or narrowed
- Entries merged or removed due to overlap
- Claims withheld because validation was insufficient

## Red Flags
- Boilerplate verification notes
- Near-duplicate concept clusters
- Claims that rely on memory or familiarity instead of checking
- Artificial quota-padding behavior
