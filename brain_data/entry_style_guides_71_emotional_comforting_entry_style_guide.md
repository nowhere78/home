# Entry Style Guide - Emotional comforting

## Purpose
- Keep archival entries consistent, information-dense, and free from filler.

## Style Rules
- Prefer specific factual phrasing over vague summary language.
- Keep entries compact but complete enough to stand alone.
- Distinguish definition, method, history, dispute, and misconception clearly when relevant.
- Do not write promotional, mystical-certainty, or pseudo-authoritative filler.
- Do not repeat the same fact in lightly reworded variants.

## Entry Skeleton
- Term/topic
- High-confidence definition
- Distinguishing detail
- Context, caution, or boundary note if needed
