## 2. The Nine Worlds of Data (Realms)
Classification into "Realms" allows the `SeiðrOrchestrator` to apply different mathematical and logical weights to information based on its nature.

### **Asgard (The High Axioms)**
*   **Definition**: Core Identity, Sigrid's Soul, Immutable Project Laws.
*   **Integrity Requirement**: 100% (No drift allowed).
*   **Validation**: Constant hash-checking against Tier 1 sources.

### **Midgard (The Physical & Historical World)**
*   **Definition**: Objective facts, geographical data, verified history.
*   **Integrity Requirement**: >90% NLI Entailment.
*   **Validation**: Cross-referenced against multiple Tier 2 curated sources.

### **Vanaheim (The Intuitive & Speculative)**
*   **Definition**: Personal opinions, vibe-based reasoning, symbolic interpretations.
*   **Integrity Requirement**: >70% Confidence.
*   **Validation**: Fluid logic; consistency with Asgardian values is more important than literal fact.

### **Svartalfheim (The Forge of Procedure)**
*   **Definition**: Code, technical documentation, structural logic.
*   **Integrity Requirement**: Absolute syntactic correctness.
*   **Validation**: Formal verification and runtime testing.

### **Jotunheim (The Chaotic External)**
*   **Definition**: Real-time user inputs, unverified web data, "Foreign" lore.
*   **Integrity Requirement**: Highly Variable.
*   **Validation**: Quarantined until verified by Tier 1/2 "Guardians."

### **Hel (The Banned & Forbidden)**
*   **Definition**: Taboos, hallucination patterns, outdated axioms.
*   **Integrity Requirement**: 0% (Banned from generation).
*   **Validation**: Aggressively filtered by the Sentinel Protocol.
