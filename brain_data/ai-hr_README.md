# Awesome-AI-HR
# AI HR Compliance Agents Ecosystem

**Curated List of SaaS Products & Open-Source GitHub Projects**  
*Focused on AI Agents for HR Compliance*  
**Last updated: March 2026**

This repository tracks notable **SaaS platforms** and **open-source projects** building **AI HR compliance agents** — autonomous or semi-autonomous systems that automate audits, policy enforcement, risk assessment, compliance checks, training, incident reporting, regulatory adherence, and remediation in HR workflows.

**Examples** include Auditocity, SafetyCulture, Rippling, Zenefits, and Compligo by KPA (the category leaders). Tools listed here emphasize **agentic capabilities** (multi-step reasoning, document analysis, real-time monitoring, automated remediation, RAG grounding on policies/laws) rather than basic checklists.

**Open-source emphasis**: This section is heavily expanded with every major active project for self-hosting, local LLMs (Ollama), custom agents, and full control over sensitive HR/employee data.

Contributions welcome! Open a PR to add/update entries. Keep descriptions factual and link to official sites.

## SaaS Products

### Core Platforms (Full AI HR Compliance Agents)

- **[Auditocity](https://auditocity.com/)**  
  AI-powered HR compliance auditing platform with a 14,000+ question library covering federal/state/industry rules. Agentic workflows deliver automated findings reports, risk prioritization, and remediation roadmaps. Ideal for self-audits and gap analysis.

- **[SafetyCulture](https://safetyculture.com/)** (formerly iAuditor)  
  Comprehensive HR compliance agent with AI-driven audits, inspections, training, and real-time reporting. Automates policy checks, incident management, and regulatory adherence across teams.

- **[Rippling](https://www.rippling.com/)**  
  Unified HR/IT/Finance platform with built-in AI compliance agents. Automates policy acknowledgments, location-specific labor law enforcement, audits, and alerts for multi-state/global teams.

- **[Zenefits](https://www.zenefits.com/)** (TriNet Zenefits)  
  All-in-one HRIS with strong AI compliance tools for payroll, benefits, and policy management. Automates compliance tasks, audits, and reporting for bundled HR operations.

- **[Compligo by KPA](https://kpa.io/platform/compligo/)**  
  Enterprise compliance management agent focused on training, audits, and policy enforcement. AI-enhanced workflows for safety, HR, and regulatory tracking in high-risk industries.

### Advanced & Specialized Agents

- **[HR Acuity](https://www.hracuity.com/)**  
  AI platform purpose-built for employee relations and HR compliance. Agentic case management, policy guidance, investigation automation, and defensible documentation.

- **[SixFifty](https://www.sixfifty.com/)**  
  AI-powered policy generator and compliance updater. Drafts/maintains employee handbooks and policies with multi-state legal intelligence and attorney-reviewed outputs.

**Other notable mentions**: Paylocity (AI compliance dashboards), Gusto (compliance automation), BambooHR (compliance add-ons), and Microsoft Copilot for HR compliance workflows.

## Open-Source GitHub Projects

### Dedicated AI HR Compliance Agent Projects

- **[AI-HR-Agent](https://github.com/eVolpe-AI/AI-HR-Agent)**  
  First free/open-source AI chatbot agent for HCM/HRMS systems (e.g., MintHCM). Handles policy questions, data changes, compliance queries, and HR workflows. Fully local and extensible.

- **[agent-for-hr-service-solution-accelerator](https://github.com/microsoft/agent-for-hr-service-solution-accelerator)**  
  Microsoft’s official open-source HR service AI agent. Summarizes policies, generates compliant HR documents, updates records, and automates employee support with full audit trails.

- **[mcp-agent](https://github.com/sanjanb/mcp-agent)**  
  Production-ready minimalist HR policy assistant using RAG + Model Context Protocol. Answers employee questions from your own policy documents with citations and audit logging. Perfect for self-hosted compliance Q&A.

- **[Veritas-AI-for-Compliance](https://github.com/llSourcell/Veritas-AI-for-Compliance)**  
  On-prem “glass-box” RAG compliance copilot. Answers GDPR/AI-Act/HR policy questions over internal documents with line-level citations, PII redaction, RBAC, and evaluation dashboards.

- **[agentic-employment](https://github.com/ruvnet/agentic-employment)**  
  Full autonomous agentic employment/HR platform. Includes compliance agents for policy enforcement, audit reporting, onboarding, and workforce management with built-in security and logging.

- **[AI_Audit_Compliance](https://github.com/hpant5/AI_Audit_Compliance)**  
  AI system that automates collection, organization, and analysis of legal/compliance data for HR audits and regulatory workflows.

- **[Autonomous-Data-Compliance-Officer-Multi-Agent-System](https://github.com/Muh76/Autonomous-Data-Compliance-Officer-Multi-Agent-System)**  
  Multi-agent system with specialized agents for risk scanning, policy matching, reporting, and HR/data compliance monitoring. Built with Google ADK + Vertex AI (adaptable to local LLMs).

- **[AI_Agents_Hackathon / hr-compliance-bot](https://github.com/nisk20/hr-compliance-bot)** (via Microsoft Hackathon)  
  Multi-agent HR compliance bot for labor laws, human rights, and occupational safety. Handles regulatory questions with Azure AI integration (fully open-source core).

- **[lola-hr-agent-showcase](https://github.com/nanangel70/lola-hr-agent-showcase)**  
  AI agent for automated HR contract management, compliance review, and workflow simplification.

- **[sable-ai-governance-framework](https://github.com/davidcockson-compliance/sable-ai-governance-framework)**  
  Open-source AI governance framework tailored for UK HR/recruitment providers. Includes risk classification, DPIA templates, bias monitoring, and compliance matrices.

### Additional Strong Open-Source Options
- **[AI-Compliance-Auditor](https://github.com/awsdataarchitect/ai-compliance-auditor)** — Serverless AI compliance auditor with audit trails (adaptable to HR policy reviews).  
- Community forks of MintHCM + AI agents for full open-source HCM compliance stacks.  
- General LangGraph/CrewAI templates for custom HR compliance agents (policy checker, audit reporter, remediation agent).

**Frameworks for building custom agents**: Use LangGraph, CrewAI, or Microsoft Semantic Kernel + python-pptx/HRIS APIs on top of these models for fully private, local HR compliance agents. See awesome-ai-agents and HR-specific RAG repos for templates.

## Disclaimer

- This is a **community-curated** list — not exhaustive and not an endorsement.
- Always verify data privacy (GDPR, CCPA, etc.), security certifications, and jurisdictional compliance when using any tool, especially with employee data.
- AI agents are powerful tools but **not substitutes** for licensed legal advice, human oversight, or official regulatory filings.
