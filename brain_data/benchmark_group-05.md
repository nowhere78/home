# Group 5: E-commerce Flow

### 5.1 View product listing
Navigate to `http://fixtures/ecommerce.html` and capture the product listing.

**Verify**: Products visible including "Wireless Headphones" at $149.99.

### 5.2 Add item to cart
Click "Add to Cart" on the "Wireless Headphones" product.

**Verify**: Cart count updates from 0 to 1.

### 5.3 View cart
Scroll down or look for the cart section showing added items.

**Verify**: Cart shows "Wireless Headphones" with price $149.99.

### 5.4 Update quantity
Find the quantity input for the cart item and change it to 2.

**Verify**: Total updates to $299.98 (2 x $149.99).

### 5.5 Remove from cart
Click the remove/delete button for the cart item.

**Verify**: Cart is empty (count shows 0).
