# YouTube Short Analysis: https://youtube.com/shorts/pWv-815AEQU

## Analysis Findings
*   **Initial Hook**: "1년만에 천만원을 15억으로 만든 천재" (Genius who turned 10M to 1.5B in 1 year). Uses high-impact financial figures and "Genius" framing.
*   **Editing Style**: Fast cuts, central dynamic captions, visual proof of the automated trading system (UI snippets).
*   **Product/Content**: "MYAISTOCK" - An automated trading program based on expert trading strategies.
*   **Conversion Strategy**: Pinned comment directing users to a Naver Cafe and providing a search keyword.

## One Big Lesson for Luna System
*   **Hook Mastery**: Always lead with a specific, high-desire outcome (the 'What') and attribute it to a unique or elite source (the 'Who' - e.g., "Genius Logic").
*   **Dual CTA**: Combine a direct link (Cafe) with a search keyword (Search 'MYAISTOCK' on Naver) to build SEO/Brand authority and bypass link friction.
