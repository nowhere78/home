This 7-day automated email sequence is designed for a high-ticket B2B financial SaaS, focusing on building authority, establishing trust, and encouraging a consultative demo rather than an immediate, impulsive purchase.
High-ticket financial SaaS requires educational content that positions the tool as an investment rather than an expense.
The 7-Day High-Ticket Finance Sequence
Goal:
Book a 15-min discovery call.
Target Persona:
CFO, VP of Finance, or Finance Director.
Day 1: The "Aha Moment" Lead Magnet Delivery
Subject:
Here is the [Report Name/Tool] for [Company Name]
Body:
Keep it short. Deliver the promised resource (whitepaper, ROI calculator, template) immediately. Position it as a way to solve a specific, expensive problem (e.g., "manual reconciliation errors" or "forecasting inaccuracies").
CTA:
No hard sell. Just, "Let me know if this helps."
Day 2: The "Expensive Problem" (Education)
Subject:
The hidden cost of [Problem]
Body:
Explain the cost of inaction. In finance, this is about risk, lost revenue, or inefficient capital allocation. Use specific figures.
Goal:
Establish authority and make them realize their current process is costing them money.
Day 3: The "Social Proof" (Case Study)
Subject:
How [Customer] reduced [Risk/Cost] by [X%]
Body:
Share a brief case study, preferably from a similar company in the same industry. Highlight the metrics and outcomes to reduce doubt.
Goal:
Build credibility and trust.
Day 4: Soft Pitch - The "Aha Moment" Product Focus
Subject:
A new way to handle [Finance Task]
Body:
Introduce how your SaaS solves the problem mentioned on Day 2. Focus on the benefit, not the feature (e.g., "Automated reconciliation" vs. "Gain 10 hours back per week").
