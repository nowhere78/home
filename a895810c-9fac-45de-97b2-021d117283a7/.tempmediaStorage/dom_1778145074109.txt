Multi-model agentic framework that converts ML research papers (PDFs) into production-ready GitHub repos. 4-stage decomposed planning, per-file analysis, self-refine loops, Docker sandbox with auto-debugging (19+ error types), CodeRAG for reference mining, and full DevOps generation (Dockerfile, CI, Makefile).
