[39] (673,331)<a class='headerlink' href='#collective-intelligence' title='Permanent link' />
Collective Intelligence
refers to systems where intelligence emerges from the interaction of multiple agents -- whether AI agents, humans, or human-AI hybrids -- achieving capabilities that exceed the sum of individual parts. While AGI focuses on single systems with general intelligence, collective intelligence explores how groups of simpler systems can coordinate, collaborate, and evolve sophisticated behaviors through interaction.
